Isekai into another fantasy world

CONTEXT

The genre of our movie is being isekai into another fantasy world. Me and my group members chose isekai to another fantasy world because we enjoyed anime and liked to see how a weak human gets stronger and defeats challenging monsters. Isekai is when you die and get resurrected to start a new life. Me and my group members had to figure out what kind of isekai we were doing. We needed to find a suitable introduction of our project but we both had different ways to start it. This granted our ability to think more deeply and how to connect both of the topics together. We thought of getting hit by a truck like normal isekai but we wanted to be more different so we decided to save a girl from a group of stalkers. We had to plan out what happens to each route you choose. This made us be creative and find out the plot of the story.

CONTENT:

This project is about you walking to the grocery store. You brought many different ingredients to prepare for dinner but you saw a girl getting stalked. Which led you to make a choice between saving the girl from a gang or not getting involved from the scene. Every choice leads you to a different opinion and restart your adventure in a new world. During your life in the new world you have to face challenges and get stronger. This led you to think of your choices and have a great adventure and make the resurrection worth it.
