Survival Love

CONTEXT

The genre of my movie was romance and horror because me and my group mate had to pick a genre but we couldn't decide. I wanted romance as the genre because I liked to watch romance shows. But my partner liked horror because she enjoys the plot of horror movies. We both had different taste in genre but decided to combine both genres together. This allowed us to develop a connection between horror and romance because when we planned the story it was very interesting. We both shared our ideas for images and what the whole movie was going to be about. Both of our thoughts were able to create this movie.

CONTENT:

My movie is about a boy and girl who never meet each other but were kidnapped and sended to different parts of the woods. Both characters' goals were to escape the woods by working together. They face different challenges throughout the movie. During the time they survive and try to escape,it causes both characters to develop feelings for each other.
